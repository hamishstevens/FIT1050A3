About The Golden Mug Cafe

Golden Mug Cafe is a unique place that combines the best Melbourne coffee-shop traditions and has a great relaxing atmosphere of Accor Hotels. Golden Mug Cafe is the perfect place for a morning cup of coffee to start the day and set the mood, or for a lunch break to have a relaxing one hour respite in a pleasant and stress-free environment.

Golden Mug Cafe has always aimed at setting the perfect atmosphere for a comfortable and stress-free time, any hour of the day. Golden Mug Cafe is also particularly distinguished by its broad menu of home-brewed coffee drinks. Whether one is a definite gourmet coffee specialist or simply an inquisitive visitor, Golden Mug Cafe has made everyone fall in love with its unique coffee culture.

Golden Mug Cafe has seeked to offer an exceptionally rich menu of coffee-based drinks, desserts and sandwiches for every taste. Golden Mug Cafe’s ultimate aim is to position itself as a special place with original coffee-brewing recipes from all over the world, perfect for a romantic date or a cosy getaway for anyone who values exceptional atmosphere and top quality service.
